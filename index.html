How it works

Simple, dignified, always open

Our pantry is a small stocked cabinet on a front lawn — like a little free library, but for pets. Anyone can stop by, anytime.

Take what you need

Grab food, treats, litter, or supplies for your pet. No forms, no proof, no questions. If your pet needs it, it's yours.

Leave what you can

Have a little extra? Drop off unopened pet food or supplies so the next neighbor finds a full shelf. Every bit helps.

It takes a village

The pantry runs entirely on neighbors looking out for one another and the animals they love. You make it possible.

Give

Two easy ways to help

Keep the shelves full and the project growing. Send supplies straight to the pantry, or chip in a few dollars — every gift feeds a furry family member.

Send supplies

Shop our Amazon Wish List — food, litter, treats, and essentials ship right to us. The easiest way to restock the shelves.

Open our Wish List

Donate cash

A few dollars buys food during shortages and helps us open more pantries. Secure giving through PayPal — no account needed.

Give with PayPal

Prefer to arrange a larger drop-off or sponsorship? Email Team@FenceHole.com — we'd love to thank you.

Visit

Find the pantry

You'll find us on the 200 block of Highland Avenue in East Pittsburgh, PA 15112 — a small pantry cabinet out front. Use the map for the general block; you'll spot the 🐾 sign when you're close.

Open 24/7. If the shelf is bare or you need something specific, just email us — we're glad to help.

The Network

One pantry became a movement

Pet parents everywhere are starting their own free pantries — and we're connecting them into one network. Find a pantry near you, or put yours on the map and join a community that's spreading kindness, block by block.

Add your pantry to the map

Started a free pet pantry — or planning to? Join the network and we'll feature it here so neighbors can find you.

Pins show your town, never an exact address. New pantries appear once we review them.

Find help anywhere

Can't make it to us? Find pet help near you.

Wherever you are, there's likely free pet food and care nearby. Share your location or type a ZIP, and we'll point you to the closest pantries plus trusted national finders. No one should face this alone.

What's next · Vet Van Fleet

From a pantry to free care for every pet

Free Pet Pantry is the first step. We're building toward Vet Van Fleet — a nonprofit initiative bringing free veterinary care to pets everywhere they are. Food keeps pets fed; healthcare keeps them well. Every pet deserves both.
